Photos

The picture above is an old photo I took. The site is Ostia Lido, it was deep nighttime after a thunderstorm and for some reason I remember perfectly it was shot on a ILFORD DELTA 3200, through a Nikkor 50 mm f/1.2 mounted on a Nikon F2 kept barely still thanks to a monopod.

I estimate the exposure time to be something near one second, looking at the walking couple portrayed. Surely I underexposed by one stop and overdeveloped by the same.

As you understand there was a time I spent much time (and money) taking pictures and developing rolls.

I still keep a Hasselblad and a Nikon 800.

There are some albums of mine on Flickr.
